Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to begin your academic journey at Punjab Technical University.

1

Check Eligibility

Review programme-specific eligibility criteria based on your academic background and entrance scores.

2

Online Registration

Register at the PTU admissions portal. Fill in personal, academic, and contact details accurately.

3

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of your mark sheets, certificates, photo ID, and passport photograph.

4

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee online via Net Banking, UPI, or Debit/Credit card.

5

Counselling & Allotment

Attend counselling (in-person or online) and receive your college/programme allotment letter.
